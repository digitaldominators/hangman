How to Play

- Press start game or join game to join a multiplayer game.

- Use the keyboard to guess a letter (I recommend starting with vowels). If your chosen letter exists in the answer, then all places in the answer where that letter appears will be revealed.

- You may be able to guess what the answer is by selecting the ‘Guess the Word’ button or fill in the remaining letters.

- The catch is every time you guess a letter wrong, you loose a life and the hangman begins to appear, piece by piece.

- Solve the puzzle before the hangman dies.

Multiplayer

- Press start game or join game to join a multiplayer game.

- Input a category & secret word or phrase that the other player will have to guess, then press Start Game.

- A code is then generated, which you can send to the other player.

- The other player will enter that code in on the Join page & also input a secret word or phrase for the category.

Game Stats

- Total score for winning on Easy is 200 pts, Medium is 400, & Hard is 600.

- Losing a game is worth 0 pts.

- Incorrect guesses is -50 pts per letter & correct guesses is 100 per letter.

- Logged in players have the option to opt in or out to display their scores on the leaderboard.

- Players without an account will be able to view their game history to view total and average scores, & games played.

- Players without an account can sign up for an account and be able to display their past/current scores.

Settings Page

Levels

- Easy: You’ll have 18 chances to guess before the stickman or skeleton is hung.

- Medium: You’ll have 10 chances to guess before the hangman is completed.

- Hard: You’ll have 6 chances to guess before the hangman is completed.

Timer

- The timer, by default, is turned off.

- You can use the Timer to set a time limit on turns for guessing during multiplayer games.

p>

- For example, if I set the timer to 10 seconds, then each player gets 10 seconds to guess the word/phrase on their turn.

- If you do not make a guess or select a letter and the timer runs out, then the other player can guess again and the timer resets.

- Don’t worry if you don’t guess within the set time - no points will be deducted!

Characters

- You can select either the original Stickman or Skeleton character to be hung.
